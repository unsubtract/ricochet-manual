What maps come with Ricochet, and how are they played?

Ricochet includes 4 maps. Here's a brief description of each, their objectives, and their scoring systems. For more information, click on the map names for an overview of the map,tips, and strategy.

 RC_Arena - One on One arena play.

Fast paced one-on-one play with additional players waiting in queue.

Number of Active Players: 2.

 

  RC_Deathmatch - Multiplayer free-for-all.

Recommended Number of Active Players: 8.

 

  RC_Deathmatch 2 - Multiplayer free-for-all.

Just be the last one standing.

Recommended Number of Active Players: 2 to server maximum.

 

RC_Arena

Objective:

Eliminate your opponent by hitting them off of their pad with your disc.

- This map plays with 2 or 4 players. Any odd-numbered players will be queued and rotated into game play until a fourth player is available.

Scoring:

1 point per kill, with 1 point bonuses for each teleport or bounce that result in a kill.

 

- Use the purple teleporter bars to hit your opponent from different angles.

- You can only kill your opponnent by knocking them completely off their pad or by decapitation .  If you hit your opponent and they do not fall from the pad, the match will continue.

RC_Deathmatch

Objective:

Eliminate your opponent by knocking them off of their pad.

 

Scoring:

1 point per kill, with 1 point bonuses for each teleport or bounce that result in a kill.

 

- For more advanced movement, use the jump pads to bounce onto walls and change your trajectory.

- Use the powerup for an advantage.
